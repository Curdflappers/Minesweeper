--BASIC GAMEPLAY--
	
Long press functionality
	Unrevealed: do opposite action (sweep vs flag)
	Revealed: highlight unrevealed neighbors (for clarity)
	


--ADVANCED GAMEPLAY--

"Dig up" mines: Remove a mine from the field, updating its neighbors' numbers

Different colored flags

Autosolver

Hints (using what the Autosolver would do)

Infinite Minesweeper

Valley Sweeper: sweep one column at a time: each column swept is cleared to reveal a new column, and mines get denser and denser

--OTHER--

Save game functionality

High score tracker

Confirm button upon resetting incomplete games
